The Unbreakable
Agastya
Chapter 1

Summary

In the streets of Gorakhpur, Saurya, brilliantly portrayed by Saugat Malla, grapples with his troubled mind, haunted by a difficult past. After 1 year of disappearance, his brother and friend, Nisan (Najir Husen), embark on a quest to find him. As Nisan delves into Saurya's history, a touching story unfolds, involving a mysterious girl named Chandani (Malika Mahat). 'The Unbreakable Agastya - Chapter 1' takes us on a journey through fractured memories and unveils the strong bond between two brothers, revealing the secrets that shaped Saurya's fragile state.
